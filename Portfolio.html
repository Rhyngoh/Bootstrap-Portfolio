About Me

My name is Ryan Ngoh. I am born and raised in Dallas, TX. I graduated from the University of Texas at Dallas with a Biology B.S in May 2016. I was studying to go into medical school but after working in the hospital as a scribe, I realized I didn't want to work in the medical setting long term. That's when I decided to move to Austin and learn full stack programming. I have always been fascinated in coding and programming and aspire to be a great developer. I hope to develop my own game in the future.

Some of my hobbies include:

  • Cooking
  • Baking
  • Weight Training
  • Video games (Mainly PC and phone)
  • Board games (Favorite games right now: Seven Wonders and Smash Up

My parents both graduated with electrical engineering degrees with my father going into software engineering. My twin brother is also studying computer science at UTD. With my change in career, I join my family as yet another programmer.
